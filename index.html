Boiler Installation

Installing a new modern central heating system is a great way to modernise a property and increase its value. Whether you are renovating a property which requires new central heating or want to replace your home central heating with a more efficient system, we can manage the entire project from start to finish. 

NHPS understand the importance of ensuring your domestic boiler installation is carried out with the upmost care and attention. A boiler is one of the most important and expensive investments you will make in your home and to ensure the domestic boiler is the correct fit for your needs and home, operates efficiently and lasts and I always advise it is installed by a reputable and qualified Gas Safe Engineer. Being a multi-skilled gas/heating engineer I can provide a solution to almost any domestic boiler problem.




Boiler Servicing

NHPS understand the importance of having a fully operational and reliable heating system in your home. I am fully trained to service several make and models of boilers and cylinders with over 15 years’ experience, so you can be assured that your heating system is in safe hands.

Book your annual boiler service with NHPS to carry out a thorough check up to ensure your boiler is working efficiently and safely. Regular servicing is the best way to take care of your boiler and help it last for many years to come and could save you the time, expense and hassle of a boiler breakdown in future.




Powerflushing

Powerflushing is a cleansing process which aim to remove deposits of sludge, rust and any other debris from your central heating system. If left, these contaminants can impact the efficiency of the system and cause irreversible damage.

NHPS use up to date powerflushing machine with magnetic filters and chemicals to breakdown and remove these contaminants from your central heating system to leave it running as efficiently as possible, prevent breakdowns and increase the lifespan of your boiler.
