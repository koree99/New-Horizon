New Horizon Production (Drone division)

Drone Surveys

Drone surveys/ Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) have become an increasingly popular method of carrying out surveying projects and by eliminating manual at-height working, they greatly reduce risk and improve safety outcomes. One of the major advantages of drone surveys is the versatility it offers your project. Suitable across a range of our services, drones are effective for land surveys and offer an excellent option for inaccessible areas.

We specialise in construction surveys for buildings and rooftops. Providing images, videos and information on to the client or relevant engineers or trade for remedial work or signing off. We also provide drodevelopment.ne surveying for other commercial applications including surveying land for property




Roof Surveys

Our roof surveys capture high quality data and footage that give you a view from above without the cost of hiring a plane or helicopter. 

Commercial and office buildings, high-rise buildings in built-up areas, industrial units, asbestos roofs, apex roofs with limited inspection space, or complex residential or historic buildings all benefit from the hands-off but highly detailed output from drone roof surveys.

Call us to inspect your roof safely for Planned Preventative Maintenance, or for reactive maintenance assessments following storm damage, falling tiles or breakages.




Drone Filming/Photography

Whether you’re a real estate agent looking for a unique perspective to showcase a property, a construction project manager seeking high-resolution site surveys, a television producer in need of captivating aerial shots, or a marketer aiming to amplify a product launch or campaign, our comprehensive drone photography services are designed to meet your diverse needs.

Most videos promoting festivals, live sports, and large business conferences in recent years have begun with aerial drone footage, and bird’s-eye shots showing the scale of events are the obvious go-to for eye-catching social media visuals. And, with large crowds, it’s vital to work with a safety-focused professional. We provide end-to-end production for corporate, trade, fundraiser, sporting, music, social, and indoor events, offering aerial and ground photography and filming.
